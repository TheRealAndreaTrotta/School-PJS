Works
Mona Lisa
The Mona Lisa is a famous painting by Leonardo da Vinci, known for the enigmatic gaze and mysterious smile of the depicted woman. Created between 1503 and 1506, the painting is admired for its beauty and Leonardo's use of sfumato technique.
The Last Supper
A mural painting by Leonardo da Vinci depicting the last supper of Jesus with his apostles. Completed around 1498. The painting is famous for its dramatic representation and Leonardo's ability to capture human emotions.
The Flying Machine
Leonardo da Vinci's flying machine is an iconic engineering work designed to mimic the flight of birds. It includes a lightweight frame with movable wings, inspired by the wing structure of birds.
